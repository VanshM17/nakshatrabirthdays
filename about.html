About Nakshatra Society

Who We Are

Nakshatra Society is a vibrant community at NSUT, New Delhi, bringing together students passionate about astronomy, science, and exploration. Our mission is to foster curiosity, learning, and collaboration through events, workshops, and projects.

Our Vision

To inspire the next generation of astronomers and space enthusiasts, and to create a welcoming space for all who look up and wonder.
